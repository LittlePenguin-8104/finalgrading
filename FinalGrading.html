About Traditional Food

Some traditional food when you come to Vietnam you have to try this food.

  1. Banh Chung

    2. Banh Chung is the oldest cake in Tet. It makes from glutinous rice and some beans and meats.

    Fold it squarely and tie it. And then boil in 70 to 80 degree in eight to ten hours.

  2. Pho Bo

    3. Pho Bo is a Vietnamese beef noodle soup that is typically made with a flavorful beef broth, rice noodles, and thinly slided beef.

    Then topped with fresh herbs, beanspouts and finished with some lime juice.

  3. Banh Mi

    4. Banh Mi in Vietnamese suisine it is often split lengthwise and filled with meat and savory ingredients like a submarine

    sandwich and served as a meal, called Banh Mi Thit.
